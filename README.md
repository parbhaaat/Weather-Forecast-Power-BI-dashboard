## 📊 Dashboard Highlights

### 🌡️ Current Weather
- Live temperature display with city switcher
- Weather condition (Clear, Cloudy, Rainy, etc.)
- Last updated timestamp for real-time accuracy

### 📅 7-Day Forecast
- Day-wise temperature forecast with trend line chart
- Visual icons for weather conditions per day

### 💨 Weather Metrics
- **Humidity** — moisture levels in the air
- **Wind Speed** — in km/h
- **Visibility** — in km
- **Pressure** — atmospheric pressure
- **UV Index** — ultraviolet radiation index
- **Precipitation** — rainfall data

### 🌿 Air Quality Index (AQI)
- Overall AQI score with a circular gauge (Good / Moderate / Poor)
- Pollutant breakdown including:
  - O3 (Ozone)
  - CO (Carbon Monoxide)
  - PM2.5
  - PM10
  - SO2
  - NO2

### 🌅 Sunrise & Sunset
- Daily sunrise and sunset times per city

### 🌧️ Chances of Rain
- Day-wise rain probability visualized as a horizontal bar chart
